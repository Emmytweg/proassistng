## ⏰ How It Works - Simple Version

### If you're looking to HIRE someone:

**Step 1: Browse & Find**

- Go to the website
- Click "Browse Talents"
- Look through available freelancers
- You can filter by what you need (skills, price range, etc.)

**Step 2: Pick Someone**

- Click on a freelancer's profile to learn more about them
- See their experience, rates, portfolio, and what others say about them
- Make sure they're a good fit

**Step 3: Submit Your Project**

- Click the "Hire" button
- Fill out a simple form explaining:
  - What you need done (project title)
  - Details about the work
  - When you want it done
  - Your budget
- Click submit

**Step 4: Make Payment**

- You'll be taken to a payment page
- Pay securely using Paystack
  - You can use credit card, debit card, or bank transfer
- Get a confirmation email

**Step 5: Start Working Together**

- Message your freelancer
- They start working on your project
- You stay in touch throughout

## 💡 Common Questions Answered

### Q: Do I need to create an account to browse or hire?

**A:** No! You can browse freelancers without logging in. You only need to provide your email when you submit a hire request.

### Q: How much does it cost to use ProAssistNG?

**A:** There's no fee to browse or use the platform. You only pay the freelancer directly for their work (via Paystack).

### Q: What if I'm not happy with a freelancer's work?

**A:** You can message the freelancer directly to discuss any issues. If there's a serious problem, you can also contact support.

### Q: How do I know freelancers are trustworthy?

**A:** All freelancers on ProAssistNG are vetted by the admin team. This means we've checked them out before they appear on the platform.

### Q: What if I can't find a freelancer with the exact skills I need?

**A:** You can:

1. Browse the available freelancers and find someone close to what you need
2. Use the Contact Us form to tell the admin what you're looking for
3. Check back later - new freelancers are added regularly

### Q: How does payment work?

**A:**

1. You hire a freelancer and submit the project
2. You're taken to Paystack (a secure payment system)
3. You pay using: credit card, debit card, or bank transfer
4. The payment is confirmed and the freelancer starts work

### Q: Is my payment secure?

**A:** Yes! Paystack is trusted and secure. Your payment information is protected.

### Q: Can I talk to a freelancer before hiring them?

**A:** Yes! Click the "Contact" button on their profile to send them a message first. This is a great way to ask questions before making a commitment.

### Q: What payment methods do I need?

**A:** You need one of the following:

- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard)
- Debit card
- Bank transfer

### Q: What happens after I pay?

**A:**

1. The freelancer gets a notification that they've been hired
2. You both get confirmation emails
3. You can start messaging the freelancer
4. They begin working on your project

## ✍️ Tips for Success

### For Clients:

**1. Write a clear project description**

- Explain exactly what you need
- The more details, the better
- Include deadline and budget

**2. Look at freelancer portfolios**

- See examples of their past work
- Make sure their style matches what you need

**3. Communicate clearly**

- Message the freelancer with questions
- Provide feedback as they work
- Be respectful and professional

**4. Start with smaller projects**

- If it's your first time, try a small project first
- See how the process works
- Build trust with a freelancer

**5. Leave feedback**

- After the project, let others know how it went
- Helps other clients choose the right freelancer

### For Businesses:

**1. Create detailed freelancer briefs**

- Save time by explaining exactly what you need
- The freelancer can give you a better price quote

**2. Build long-term relationships**

- Hire the same freelancer for multiple projects
- You'll get faster turnaround and better quality

**3. Plan ahead**

- Don't wait until the last minute
- Give freelancers time to do good work

**4. Budget appropriately**

- Cheaper isn't always better
- Good quality work is worth the investment

**5. Use the messaging feature**

- Stay in contact during the project
- Address any issues early
